About Me

I'm Harshit Arya, a passionate Java Backend Developer and MERN Stack Developer from Madhya Pradesh. Currently, I'm working at Indew Technology, where I build scalable web applications using cutting-edge technologies. I hold a B.Tech degree in IT from Jawaharlal Institute of Technology and am constantly exploring ways to innovate in the tech space. My journey in the tech world began with a love for solving complex problems. Over the years, I have gained expertise in Spring Boot, Hibernate, and web development. I am currently diving deeper into full-stack development with the MERN stack, and I strive to develop systems that make a real impact. Whether it's backend systems or building intuitive user experiences, I enjoy tackling challenges and bringing ideas to life. I am committed to continuous learning and am always looking for opportunities to grow both technically and personally.
